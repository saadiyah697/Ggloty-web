Everything you need to know about Ggloty, creator verification, doorstep appointments, and app features.

Ggloty is an elite beauty curation platform that connects you directly with document-verified makeup artists, hairstylists, and beauty professionals delivered straight to your doorstep. Browse real transformation reels, choose signature styles, and schedule appointments on your terms.

Traditional directories rely on static text listings and outdated photos. Ggloty features an interactive Feed of real video reels and a curated Lookbook of verified creator portfolios, giving you complete visibility into an artist’s skills, real client reviews, and transparent pricing before booking.

Simply download the Ggloty Mobile App, explore the Feed or Lookbook to select your preferred style or creator, select a date and time slot, and confirm. Your assigned creator will arrive at your location fully equipped with professional products.

We are actively launching across Tamil Nadu and expanding into major metropolitan hubs across India. You can book instant doorstep sessions based on live creator availability or schedule appointments weeks in advance for weddings, corporate events, pitch meetings, or special occasions.

Pricing is 100% transparent and displayed upfront in the app prior to checkout—with zero hidden service or travel charges. Payments are processed securely inside the Ggloty app via UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, or digital wallets.

Every creator undergoes a multi-tier vetting process, including government ID verification, background checks, and physical portfolio reviews. All artists follow strict sanitation standards, using sanitized tools and single-use applicators where appropriate.

You can apply directly through the Ggloty Mobile App by tapping "Apply as a Creator" or by submitting your portfolio, credentials, and work samples to creators@ggloty.com.

Joining Ggloty gives you direct exposure to high-intent clients nearby, complete independence over your schedule and service pricing, and a high-end platform to showcase your artistry through video reels and lookbook features.
